My name is Yixuan Liu and you can just call me Eric. I am a fourth-year student majoring in Interactive Arts & Technology at SFU. I hope that my interest and passion for UX design will lead to better designs that bring convenience and better experiences to users in their daily lives. Therefore, user surveys and feedback, as well as data analysis, are important to me because they provide accurate direction for design, which I believe is the most important step in user-centered design. I can apply my skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Figma, and Final Cut to help me to complete the design to the satisfaction of users.
